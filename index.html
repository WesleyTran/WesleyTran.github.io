  •   Team-Building Coordination  |  Team-Leadership Experience

    As a team member, I always have my eyes set on empowering and bringing people together in a project as a form of leadership. This achievement was able to create a closer team bond between the support and development team, allowing for quicker development testing. It is made evident through my ability to communicate succinctly with people with various levels of technical proficiency and different personality types.

    Being new to the company and seeing an opportunity, I took initiative and proposed team building activities for all participants to be involved with support from management. I researched, planned, and trialled different activities such as verbal icebreakers, online virtual activities and card games with each individual member and reflected upon each experience every time. This required testing designation of agreed allocated time slots for these activities- after long meetings and during start/end of days and required in the inclusion of all without others feeling excluded. These activities specialised in highlighting communication between different members, spotlighting their individual strengths and strengthened the relationship between the members. Despite being out of my responsibilities, I was able to take initiative and motivate many engineers through activities, the main challenge being, working with individuals who were uninterested or had tough schedules.

    My tried and tested solution: shorter games through longer days and longer games after long meetings.
    I was able to master how to effectively empower individuals and consistently highlight and showcase their abilities.

    I am an individual who seeks growth through critique and maintaining accountability for my actions as an aspect of leadership. Through my experience in testing, I demonstrated a growth mindset, having the ability to accept and follow up my mistakes.
